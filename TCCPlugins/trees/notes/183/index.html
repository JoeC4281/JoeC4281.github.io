The Unicode fullwidth characters tend to be fatter than their ASCII equivalents. (Hence the name, I guess.)

They do have the handy property of being remapped to regular ASCII characters if you redirect to a non-Unicode file. Or to a non-Unicode stream:

Code:

C:\Bin\JPSDK\SafeChars>input /L75 `>>> ` %%myline
>>> A&&||Z>


C:\Bin\JPSDK\SafeChars>set myline=%@safeenv[myline]


C:\Bin\JPSDK\SafeChars>echo %myline
A＆＆｜｜Z＞


C:\Bin\JPSDK\SafeChars>echo %myline >>:a con:
A&&||Z>


C:\Bin\JPSDK\SafeChars>
