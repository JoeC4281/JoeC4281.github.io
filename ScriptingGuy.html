The Best (And the Rest) of Hey, Scripting Guy!

 

 

There is a help file, called hey_archive.chm, that contains a copy of all the columns that "Hey, Scripting Guy!" has published during the past three years.

 

From what year, I am not sure.

 

As of 2023/01/28,

the file hey_archive.chm is available from

 

https://www.skybadger.net/scripting/platform/

 

The hey_archive.chm file will be blocked after downloading,

thus you will have to Unblock it from the file properties dialog.

 

 

 

There are some another files,

article_archive.chm,

script_center.chm,

similar to hey_archive.chm,

with some articles duplicated.
